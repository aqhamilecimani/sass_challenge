Chicken breast with Spinach & Cheese

Splitting chicken breasts and filling them with your favorite flavors makes them anything but ordinary. Here spinach, two kinds of cheese, and garlic come together to stuff golden chicken breasts with a flavorful, cheesy filling.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless,skinless chicken breast

  • 1 teaspoom kosher salt, divided

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature

  • 2 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese (about 1/2 cup)

  • 1 box frozen spinach, thawed and squeezed to remove excess moisture

  • 1/2 small sweet onion, grated

  • 1 clove garlic, minced

    • Equipment

  • Measuring cup and spoons

  • Chef's knife and cutting board

  • 12-inch skillet or cast iron pan

  • Small mixing bowl

  • Paper towels

    • Instructions

    Make a pocket in the chicken breasts and season

    Make the filling

    Stuff the chicken breasts with the filling, spooning a quarter of the filling mixture into each breast.

    Heat the oil over medium-high heat in a large skillet

    Carefully add the chicken to the hot pan and cook for 5 to 7 minutes

    Flip the chicken and cook until it reaches 165Â°F, 5 to 7 minutes more

    Slice and serve.
